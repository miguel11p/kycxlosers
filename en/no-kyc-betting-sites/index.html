On betting sites without verification, this requirement disappears entirely: you can create an account, deposit funds and start betting within minutes without having to send photographs of your ID, utility bills or selfies with your passport.

I have verified first-hand that these platforms work primarily with cryptocurrencies such as Bitcoin or USDT, which allows them to operate under international licences (Curaçao, Anjouan, etc.) without applying strict European identity verification regulations.
